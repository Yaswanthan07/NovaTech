Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with AutoBid AI?

Simply sign up on our platform, upload your RFP documents, and our AI agents will automatically analyze and generate bids for you. It's that easy!

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, and direct links to tender websites. Our system can extract data from any of these formats.

How long does bid generation take?

Most bids are generated within 2-4 hours. Complex RFPs may take longer, but you'll receive updates throughout the process.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with all data protection regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties.

Can I customize the bid parameters?

Absolutely! You can set custom pricing models, margin preferences, and technical specifications for each bid.

What support options are available?

We offer 24/7 email support, phone support during business hours, and a dedicated account manager for enterprise clients.
